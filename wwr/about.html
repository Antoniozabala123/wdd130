Our Mission

Our mission is to connect people with the power of nature, providing safety and adrenaline with every stroke. Paddle with passion, live with power.

History

It all began in the summer, when three friends and an old inflatable raft adventured in the unknown on the waters of the river. What started as a personal challenge to master the rapids transformed into Venezuela Rafting Experience, a paradise for adrenaline. Since then, we have guided thousands of brave souls through the deepest canyons of the Andes, always keeping the same promise.
