Venezuela Rafting Experience

Our mission is to connect people with the power of nature, providing safety and adrenaline with every stroke. Paddle with passion, live with power. We believe that every rapid tells a story and we're here to help you write yours.

History

It all began in the summer of 2010, when three friends and an old inflatable raft adventured into the unknown on the waters of the Santo Domingo River. What started as a personal challenge to master the rapids transformed into Venezuela Rafting Experience, a paradise for adrenaline seekers. Since then, we have guided thousands of brave souls through the deepest canyons of the Andes, always keeping the same promise: adventure with the highest safety standards.

Today, we operate on five different rivers across Venezuela, offering everything from family-friendly floats to extreme class IV-V rapids for experienced adventurers. Our team of certified guides brings over 50 years of combined experience, ensuring that your safety and enjoyment come first on every trip.
